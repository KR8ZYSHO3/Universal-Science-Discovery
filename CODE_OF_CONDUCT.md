# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We participate in good faith: curiosity, precision, and respect for people and evidence. Harassment, bad-faith argument, or personal attacks are not acceptable.

## Expected behavior

- Anchor disagreements in methods, data, and citations.
- Credit others’ ideas; surface limitations and conflicts openly.
- Protect privacy and follow [docs/ETHICS_REPRODUCIBILITY_AND_DATA.md](docs/ETHICS_REPRODUCIBILITY_AND_DATA.md) for sensitive information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Includes, but is not limited to: harassment, discriminatory conduct, doxxing, sustained disruption of discussion, and knowingly misrepresenting results.

## Reporting

Use **private** channels only (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and GitHub private vulnerability reporting if enabled). Do not use public issues for conduct matters that need confidentiality.

Maintainers will review reports promptly and may remove content, lock threads, or restrict access consistent with platform rules.

## Attribution

This conduct guide is adapted from common open-source community norms and may evolve by maintainer decision with repository history preserved.
